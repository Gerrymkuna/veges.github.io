From plant to plate

We offer locallymade food sourced from locally grown ingredients.

Rastan herb farm and store we pride ourselves in growing superior quality field and greenhouse fruits and vegetables. We presently operate a 3-acre greenhousethat is heated by two natural gas boilers. In the greenhouse, crops are grown biologically; meaning that we introduce natural predators instead of spraying chemical pesticides to kill the pests. The common pests found on peppers are aphids. The predators used to target these pests are purchased from around the world and are introduced into the greenhouse on a weekly basi. We choose our variety of tomatoes carefully so that our customers receive the maximum flavor. Long English cucmbers , mini cucmbers, eggplant, beans, carrots are also grown in the greenhouse. Greenhouse harvest begins in February and continues until Decemberwhen we clean out the greenhouses to replant.

Local farmers strength

Working ourselves was not our target but we have a big cooperation with the local outsiders, we connect our farms and ideas to the local farmer around our community and try to empower them with ne technology and farming skills. Our main goal is to change the farming system from the farms to the flow of products in the markets by using the sustainable means. We make daily follow up on the local farms from the process of sustainable replanting of the fruits and vegetables, sustainable growth and care and finalizing with harvesting seasons.
